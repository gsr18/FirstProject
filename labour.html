Farm Labourer's Page

  • Laborers can register for work on vegetable, fruit, cereal, spice, or dairy farms.
  • Option to work with agricultural machinery or in agricultural transportation.
  • Reduces the need to migrate for work; connects laborers to local farmers.
  • Supports stability in local jobs and helps address labor shortages.
